Why Choose Ahava?

Ahava Medicare provides a convenient and cost effective telemedicine service for healthcare providers, patients and their families. Built on the cutting edge of technology, Ahava Medicare helps improve access to care while also reducing wait times.

Learn More

Mission Statement

To provide a better approach to personalized patient care by eliminating the burden of unnecessary hospital appointments, reduce the burden on primary care physicians and enable direct access to specialists through specialist referrals.

Vision Statement

Our practice focuses on enhancing the quality of care of our patients and obtaining positive clinical outcomes. Also, we hope to improve the quality of life of our patients by actively involving them in their management plans.

Value Statement

We are a faith-based international telemedicine company that takes pride in our uncompromising integrity and honesty. We believe that with love and gentle care, we can improve the quality of life of our patients.

Services

As a regular customer or a first-time user of our medical services, you will be able to enjoy the following benefits:

Medical Consultation

A holistic approach to medical concerns of patients across all age groups, including teens, young adults, and older populations. We provide lab investigation reports interpretations and provide the clinical significance of this report. In addition, we ensure our patients understand their health conditions and the implications of their lifestyle on their overall health status.

Chronic Care Management

Consistent education concerning the complexities of living with various chronic illnesses. We use assessment and interventions to help patients maintain a high quality of life regardless of their chronic illnesses. Some of the relevant chronic conditions include; Osteoarthritis, Diabetes, Hypertension, Peptic ulcer disease, Asthma, Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs), abnormal vaginal or penile discharge, pelvic inflammatory disease.

Preventive Medicine

We ensure that our patients are up-to-date with necessary vaccinations and immunizations. Also, we educate our patients on the importance of lifestyle modifications in managing various disease conditions such as asthma, diabetes, hypertension, and osteoarthritis.

Lifestyle & Prevention

We ensure that our patients are up-to-date with necessary vaccinations and immunizations. Also, we educate our patients on the importance of lifestyle modifications in managing various disease conditions such as asthma, diabetes, hypertension, and osteoarthritis.

Doctor-Patient Interaction

A multi-disciplinary team is available to answer medically related questions and provide clarity on health-related concerns.

Specialist Care Referrals

We act as a bridge between our patients and specialists. We provide referrals for patients in need of specialist care to top-tier hospitals. Some conditions in need of specialist care include: Congestive heart failure, Chronic liver disease, Chronic kidney disease, Atrial fibrillation, Diabetes mellitus complications, Hypertensive heart disease, Cancer, Thyroid disorders, Autoimmune diseases, Infertility, Uterine fibroids, Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH), Hemorrhoids, Hernia, Appendicitis

Pricing List of our Concierge Private Medical Services

Basic Plan (N 2,500 For Each Consultation)

All primary care related consultations and treatments
This includes:

  • 9 – 5 access to your doctor via email, telephone, or text messages.
  • 1 voice call consultation of 10 minutes per month (on appointment basis).
  • Review of lab investigations.
  • Education and counseling on preventive medicine.

Intermediate Plan (N 10,000 for 14 days)

All primary care related consultations and treatments
This includes:

  • 9 – 5 access to your doctor via email, telephone, or text messages.
  • 2 voice call consultations of 10 minutes (on appointment basis).
  • Review of lab investigations and results.
  • Referrals for specialist care or examinations.
  • Education and counseling on preventive medicine.

Advanced Plan (N 20,000 for 14 days)

All primary care related consultations and treatments
This includes:

  • 9 – 9 access to your doctor via email, telephone, or text messages.
  • 2 voice call consultations of 20 minutes (on appointment basis)
  • 1 video call consultation of 10 minutes (on appointment basis).
  • Review of lab investigations and results.
  • Referrals for specialist care or examinations.
  • Education and counseling on preventive medicine.

Family Plan (N 50,000 for 28 days)

All primary care related consultations and treatments.
This includes:

  • Care of a nuclear family (Father, Mother, and Children).
  • 9 – 5 access to your doctor via email, telephone, or text messages.
  • 1 video call consultation of 20 minutes (on appointment basis).
  • Review of lab investigations and results.
  • Follow up and monitoring of treatment plan.
  • Education and counseling on preventive medicine.
  • Ensuring up to date vaccination and immunization of the family.
  • Referrals for specialist care or examinations.
